I respect elderly people who reinvent themselves in modern times. For example, I went back to school at the astounding age of 33.

While working, or driving for that matter, I prefer to listen to classical compositions, or even medieval European music.

Human connection is in my opinion of upmost importance, so I try to be approachable, respectful and helpful. Comedy is a necessity.

To create some contrast I frequently go hiking deep in the woods, equipped to sit down, cook something and enjoy the serenity.

A stranger is just a possible friend you haven’t met yet. However, my best friend will always be my pupper. Well, he’s more of a mutt.

I love how IT evolved to be a industry in which interaction and teamwork make the dream work.

Born and raised in the Belgian countryside in the 90’s, I’ll always be grateful for having spent my youth outdoors. In secondary school, my favourite subjects were languages, IT and history. Despite prioritising football over studying, I was a relative good student. A major injury however compelled me to change routes later in life, thus I decided to reinvent myself and invest my time and resources in relevant and future-proof education.

IT, and technology in general, has always interested me, however my first true concrete experience was just a few years ago, when I applied for a 1 year course of Full Stack Development. Finishing this succesfully, and having really enjoyed it, I decided to commit fully and acquire a Graduate in Programming as well.

Programming clean and responsive applications or websites is very satisfactory to me. However, what I find most exciting is creating appealing, practical and dynamic user experiences. I love how IT evolved to be a industry in which interaction and teamwork make the dream work.
